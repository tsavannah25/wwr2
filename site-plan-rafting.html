Overview

Purpose

Our website exists to promote our whitewater rafting business by showcasing our exciting guided trips, providing essential safety and booking information, and helping potential customers learn about the unforgettable adventure experiences we offer. Our goal is to build revenue through online bookings while sharing our passion for outdoor recreation and river conservation.

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure-seeking individuals and groups, ranging from young adults (ages 18â€“35) looking for an adrenaline rush, to families and corporate teams interested in outdoor bonding experiences. These customers typically have moderate to high disposable income and are interested in outdoor recreation, fitness, and travel. Most of our visitors will access the site via mobile devices while traveling or planning on-the-go, so our site will be fully mobile-responsive to support quick navigation and booking.
