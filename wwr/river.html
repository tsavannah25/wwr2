Salmon River

Winding through deep canyons and remote wilderness, the Salmon River offers world-class whitewater with a side of serenity. Rapids, hot springs, and history all in one unforgettable ride.

The Green River

From towering red rock cliffs to playful Class III rapids, the Green River is a favorite for scenic floats and family-friendly adventure through Canyonlands country.
